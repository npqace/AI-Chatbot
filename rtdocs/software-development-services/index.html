CUSTOM SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT LIFECYCLE

  • Planning
    We collaborate closely with our clients to gather all the necessary information and requirements, ensuring that our custom software development solutions align with their expectations.
  • Designing
    Our team prepares system and document designs based on the specified requirements, allowing us to define the overall system architecture and technology stack.
  • Defining
    Once the requirement analysis is complete, we proceed to define and document the specific software needs.
  • Building
    Our skilled developers begin building the entire system by writing code using selected programming languages, techniques, and methodologies.
  • Testing
    We conduct testing to evaluate the software quality, aiming to identify and address any defects.
  • Deployment
    The final software is released and meticulously checked for any deployment issues.
  • Maintenance
    As per the agreed service level agreement, we ensure that ongoing needs are met and the system continues to perform according to the initial specifications outlined in the planning phase.
